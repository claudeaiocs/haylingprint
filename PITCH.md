# Hayling Print App — Sales Pitch

## The Opportunity

Hayling Print is a quality print and design studio with a solid product range — stationery, banners, booklets, signage, clothing, merchandise, wedding print, and more. You've got an online ordering system, which puts you ahead of many local printers. But there's a massive gap between a website and a mobile app — and that gap is costing you orders.

## The Problem

- **Proof approval is slow** — email back and forth, customers forget to check, delays pile up
- **Reordering is friction** — regular customers have to go through the full order process every time
- **"Where's my order?" calls** eat up staff time that could be spent printing
- **No push marketing** — website visitors come once, you can't reach them again
- **Artwork uploads are messy** — wrong formats, low resolution, missing bleed = rework
- **Mobile ordering** — customers browse on phones but your site may not be optimised for quick mobile purchases

## The Solution: The Hayling Print App

An app that makes ordering print as easy as ordering a takeaway. Browse, configure, upload, approve, track — all from a phone.

## Key Features

### 1. Product Configurator 🛒
Every product, fully configurable on mobile. Business cards? Choose size, paper, finish, quantity — see the price update live. Banners? Pick material, size, eyelets, hemming. No more "can you quote me for...?" emails. Self-service, instant pricing.

### 2. Artwork Upload with Validation 📤
Customers upload directly from their phone — camera, gallery, or cloud storage. The app checks the file before submission:
- ✅ Resolution OK (300dpi+)
- ✅ Correct dimensions
- ⚠️ No bleed detected — add 3mm
- ❌ File is RGB, needs CMYK

**Result:** Fewer rejected files, less rework, faster turnaround.

### 3. Proof Approval — The Game Changer ✅
This is where you save HOURS per week.

**Current process:** Design proof → email to customer → customer doesn't check email → you chase → they reply "looks good" three days later → you've lost three days.

**App process:** Proof uploaded → push notification "Your proof is ready!" → customer opens app → zooms in, checks it → taps APPROVE or leaves comments → you get it back in minutes, not days.

Comments directly on the proof: "Make the logo bigger", "Wrong phone number", "Can we try blue instead?" Version history keeps everything tidy.

### 4. Order Tracking 📦
Real-time status updates:
- ✅ Order Received
- 🎨 Artwork Check
- 🖨️ Printing
- ✂️ Finishing
- 📦 Dispatched
- 🚚 Out for Delivery

Push notification at every stage. Customers know exactly where their order is without picking up the phone. **Fewer "where's my order?" calls = more time printing.**

### 5. One-Tap Reorder 🔄
This is the loyalty machine. A business that ordered 500 business cards three months ago? They open the app, tap "Reorder", confirm, done. Same specs, same artwork, 10 seconds.

You're making it so easy to reorder that they'd never go anywhere else. Why would they when it's one tap?

### 6. Quick Quote 💬
Bespoke job? Customer describes what they need, uploads a reference image, gets a quote back via the app. In-app chat for back-and-forth. Professional, tracked, nothing lost in email threads.

### 7. Push Notification Marketing 🔔
This is revenue you're currently not getting:
- "Spring Sale: 20% off all banners this week!"
- "Wedding season — order save-the-dates now"
- "Your last business card order was 6 months ago — time to restock?"
- "NEW: Recycled product range now available"
- "1 Day Express available for orders placed before 10am"

You're in their pocket, year-round. No relying on them remembering to visit your website.

### 8. Trade Account Management 🏷️
B2B customers log in and see their trade pricing. Order history, invoices, account balance — all in-app. Download invoices as PDF. Makes you look like a national operation while staying local and personal.

## Why Flutter?

- **One codebase** → iOS + Android simultaneously
- **Fast development** → MVP in 6-8 weeks
- **Native performance** → Camera uploads, push notifications, smooth UI
- **Cost effective** → Half the price of building two separate apps

## Phased Approach

**Phase 1 (MVP):** Product catalogue + ordering + artwork upload + order tracking
**Phase 2:** Proof approval + push notifications + reorder
**Phase 3:** Trade accounts + quick quote chat + marketing automation
